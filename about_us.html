About Us

At the Velvet Bite Bakery We aren't here to revolutize the baked goods space. We are just here to provide good deserts not healthy not gluten free not low calorie just good tasty deserts. This all started when we were at our grandmas house and she would make the best cookies in the world and when we gave it to our freinds we all agreed it was the best cookies we ever had. Over the years we have gotten all of her old recipes and even made some of our own and we are going to share those with you!
